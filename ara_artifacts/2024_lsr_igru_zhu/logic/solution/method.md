## Abstract or digest

Stock price prediction is a challenging problem in the field of fi- nance and receives widespread attention. In recent years, with the rapid development of technologies such as deep learning and graph neural networks, more research methods have begun to focus on exploring the interrelationships between stocks. However, existing methods mostly focus on the short-term dynamic relationships of stocks and directly integrating relationship information with temporal information. They often overlook the complex nonlin- ear dynamic characteristics and potential higher-order interaction relationships among stocks in the stock market.
